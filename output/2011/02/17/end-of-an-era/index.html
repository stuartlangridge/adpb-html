End of an era

Referring to the decree made in this cause on the 21st September 2010, whereby it was decreed that the marriage solemnised on the 3rd May 1998.

at The Maids Head Hotel, Tombland, Norwich in the District of Norwich, in the County of Norfolk.

between Samantha Elaine Langridge the Petitioner

and Stuart Ian Langridge the Respondent

be dissolved unless sufficient cause be shown to the court within six weeks from the making thereof why the said decree should not be made absolute, and no such cause having been shown, it is hereby certified that the said decree was on the 20th January 2011, made final and absolute and that the said marriage was hereby dissolved.

I didn’t expect to feel quite this sad.
