Minimal community

Having a play with Inkscape this evening, to see what it’s like, and I had this odd idea. So, minimalist posters for our beloved Ubuntu Community team.

Much help from OpenClipArt and others on this, specifically wildchief, Anonymous, GMcGlinn, RenOliver, ben, DragonArtz Designs, and Wikipedia. It’s nice to be able to draw SVGs, so also big thanks Inkscape, and all the people who did tutorials about perspective transforms and so on.
